Requirements

Participating buyers and sellers must agree to the Terms and Conditions.

To Sell Textbooks

1. Fill out the Sellers’ Application and bring the books to Textbook Mango office.
2. Books submitted would be listed on Textbook Mango Trade Centre website for free.
3. Get paid for the books you sold!

To Buy Textbooks

Simply turn up to the office during the business hour (Mon-Fri, 9am-5pm) or order and pay on website.

Cost/Reimbursement

Listing with Textbook Mango Trade Centre is absolutely FREE. Only when your book successfully sells, we will charge a transaction fee of 8% based on selling price per book sold.

All reimbursements will be done via internet banking and deposited into the bank account specified in the Sellers’ Application .
